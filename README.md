# Cerulean-chat

User-friendly unofficial whatsapp companion client with no ads, no data collection, but extra flavor! 🌶️

## Why Cerulean?

In addition to standard messaging, Cerulean provides:

* **Universal Search:** Enhanced search functionality to find users outside your contacts.
* **Chat Bubbles:** Seamless multitasking with message overlays that allow you to chat over any application.
* **Advanced Presence:** Complete control over your visibility; toggle between appearing always online or remaining completely hidden.
* **Data Persistence:** Deleted or expired content remain available until you choose to remove them.
* **Improved Receipts:** Granular privacy settings to manage one-sided or mutual read receipts for individual contacts.
* **Customised Alerts:** Real-time notifications for specific events, including message delivery and read confirmations.
* **Offline Mode:** Restrict internet access while most of the features remain usable.
